Pancakes

These are the best buttermilk pancakes out there. You will never look back once you make these fluffy pancakes.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 Tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 3/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups buttermilk
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • 2 Tablespoons melted butter
  • 2 Tablesspoons milk, to thin batter

Steps

  1. Heat griddle to 350 degrees F. Grease with butter or non-stick cooking spray.
  2. Combine dry ingredients in large bowl: flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, salt.
  3. Add buttermilkm eggs and melted butter and mix just until wet and dry ingredients are incorporated.
  4. Add milk, a little at a time until batter is still thick, but pourable.
  5. Use a 1/4 cup measuring cup to scoop batter and pour on hot griddle. Cook pancakes until bubbles appear on the surface. Then Flip.
  6. Serve warm with syrup!
